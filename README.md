# AI Powered Study Buddy

AI Powered Study Buddy is like a personal study partner available anytime you need help. It helps students understand difficult topics, shorten long notes into simple points, and practice with quizzes before exams. Instead of struggling alone, students can enter a topic or paste their notes and quickly get easy explanations, quick summaries, and practice questions.

## Features

* **Easy Explanations**
  Breaks down tough concepts into simple language.

* **Quick Summaries**
  Turns long paragraphs into short, clear key points.

* **Practice Questions**
  Creates quiz questions to test understanding and improve revision.

## How It Works

Just type a topic or paste your notes into the app.
The system reads the content and instantly gives you explanations, summaries, or quiz questions on the screen.

## Usage

1. Open the app
2. Enter a topic or paste your notes
3. Choose what you need (explanation, summary, or quiz)
4. Review and study the generated content

## Project Purpose

The goal of this project is to make studying easier and more engaging. It shows how AI can support students by saving time, clearing doubts quickly, and helping them prepare better for exams.
